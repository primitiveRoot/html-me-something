The Data is In

Since January, data collected from large internet service providers and search engines indicates an increased interest in data. "what is data" ranked among the top searches since March of this year, followed by "what is exponential" and "is data a conspiracy".
This is a marked change from recent years. Largely, interest in data has been limited to a few outliers. Data had failed to capture the public imagination because it is perceived as "boring" or "too nerdy". But, in light of recent world events, interest in data has gathered momentum. Sources speculate that "people are starting to realize that policy decisions should be based in reality."

Data Scientists are not yet sure how to use the data, but most agree that the data is in.

"Humans concerned", Scientists Worry

The announcement came after much consideration by the a task force of mathmaticians, psychologists, medical doctors, and Instagram models.

A leading expert commented, "People are increasingly concerned. This is no cause for alarm; however, if people become alarmed, that may be cause for concern."
